Richest 10% own almost half Canada's wealth, Broadbent Institute says

The gap between people at the top of Canada's wealth pyramid and those at the bottom is widening and showing no signs of stopping, a major left-leaning think-tank says. In a report out Thursday, the Broadbent Institute looked at the most recent Statistics Canada data on wealth levels of Canadians.
